1. 1687 - Newton Publishes "Principia Mathematica"

    Foundation of classical mechanics

  2. 1869 - Mendeleev's Periodic Table

    Organized elements by atomic weight.

  3. 1905 - Einstein's Annus Mirabilis

    Published papers on photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and mass-energy equivalence.

  4. 1928 - Discovery of Penicillin

    Alexander Fleming discovered the first true antibiotic.

  5. 1953 - Discovery of DNA Structure

    Watson and Crick proposed the double helix structure of DNA.
