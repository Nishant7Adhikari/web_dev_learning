Newton's First Law

An object remains at rest or in uniform motion unless acted upon by a net force.

Chemical Bond

A chemical bond is the force holding atoms together in a molecule.

Quadratic Formula

The solutions of ax² + bx + c = 0 are x = (-b ± √(b²-4ac)) / 2a.

Acceleration

Rate of change of velocity with time.

Atomic Number

Number of protons in the nucleus of an atom.

Frictional force

When a solid body moves in the surface of another body an opposing force comes into play between their surface of contact which is known as Frictional force.
